Could Verstappen replace Piastri at McLaren?

By Azra · Opinion · 4 min read

Rumours have been spreading that Oscar could join Red Bull while Verstappen would move to McLaren. Team Principal Andrea Stella has rejected this idea, stating that McLaren is in its best state right now with their current line-up.

That isn’t particularly surprising. McLaren has been doing great since 2023 after Oscar joined the team. Winning two Constructors' Championships isn’t exactly a reason to switch up drivers. Of course, things can always change in F1, but after hearing Lando Norris’ opinion, it most likely won’t happen soon.

“To be honest, a lot of drivers want to come to McLaren, so I don’t know why you just highlight Max! There are quite a few others that I know who want to come as well.”

Lando Norris clearly doesn’t think that such changes should be made right now. He states that he and Piastri work well together, which gives McLaren little reason to consider switching drivers.

For now, this rumour feels more like paddock noise than a realistic move. McLaren already has a strong pairing, and unless something major changes, replacing Piastri does not seem like the obvious choice.
